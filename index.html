Disclaimer

PowerLift Live is an unofficial, independent tool created to help coaches, athletes, and families follow meet results. It is not the official scoring app for any competition and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or supported by the Michigan High School Powerlifting Association (MHSPL). All competition data is sourced directly from the meet's Google Sheet. For official results, always refer to the meet director or official MHSPL resources.
